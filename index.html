Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our free digital marketing tools

What free digital marketing tools does Explainur offer? +

Explainur offers 8 free browser-based marketing tools: Meta Tag Generator for SEO optimization, Word Counter with reading time estimation, UTM Link Builder for campaign tracking, Social Media Character Counter for Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook, Color Palette Generator for branding, Open Graph Preview for social sharing, Headline Analyzer for better click-through rates, and Lorem Ipsum Generator for placeholder text. All tools work instantly without requiring signup.

How do I generate SEO meta tags for my website? +

To generate SEO meta tags, use our free Meta Tag Generator. Enter your page title (keep it between 50-60 characters), write a compelling meta description (150-160 characters), add your page URL, and include an image URL for social sharing. The tool automatically generates complete meta tags including Open Graph tags for Facebook and Twitter Card tags. Simply copy the generated code and paste it into your website's HTML head section.

What is a UTM link and how do I create one? +

A UTM link is a URL with tracking parameters that help you identify which marketing campaigns drive traffic to your website. To create one, use our UTM Link Builder. Enter your website URL, specify the source (e.g., google, facebook, newsletter), select the medium (e.g., cpc, email, social), and optionally add a campaign name. The tool creates a trackable URL you can use in your marketing materials. When users click it, Google Analytics records the source, medium, and campaign data.

Are Explainur's marketing tools really free? +

Yes, absolutely! All 8 marketing tools on Explainur are 100% free to use. No signup is required, no credit card is needed, and there are no usage limits or hidden fees. We believe powerful marketing tools should be accessible to everyone, from freelancers and small businesses to large marketing teams. All tools run directly in your browser, ensuring your data stays private and secure.

What is the character limit for Twitter posts? +

Twitter (now X) has a 280 character limit per post. Our Social Media Character Counter shows your character count in real-time and warns you when you're approaching the limit. The tool also supports other platforms: Instagram captions allow up to 2,200 characters, LinkedIn posts have a 3,000 character limit, and Facebook posts can be up to 63,206 characters. This helps you optimize content for each platform's best practices.

How accurate is the word counter reading time estimate? +

Our Word Counter calculates reading time based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute for comprehension. For technical or complex content, readers may take longer. The tool also counts total words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs, giving you comprehensive text analytics perfect for blog posts, essays, and content optimization.

How does the headline analyzer score work? +

The Headline Analyzer scores your headlines from 0-100 based on several factors: Word count (optimal: 6-12 words), presence of numbers (headlines with numbers perform 36% better), power words (emotional trigger words like "amazing," "secret," "proven"), and emotional impact. A score of 70+ indicates a strong headline that should drive good engagement and click-through rates.

Is my data safe when using these tools? +

Absolutely! Privacy is our top priority. All Explainur tools run entirely in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This means your data is processed locally on your device and never sent to our servers. We don't track your usage, store your inputs, or share your information with third parties. You can use our tools with complete confidence that your marketing data remains private and secure.
